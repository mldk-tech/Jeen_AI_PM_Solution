Enterprise AI Workspace Strategy

This dashboard outlines the proposed solution for Jeen.ai to address data fragmentation and empower data-driven decision-making through an on-premise, secure AI platform.

🚩 The Problem Space

Enterprises face a critical bottleneck: scattered data across disconnected systems prevents cohesive decision-making. Furthermore, standalone AI tools fail to demonstrate ROI because they exist outside the standard business workflow.

🗃

Fragmented Data

Information is siloed in ERPs, CRMs, emails, and messaging apps.

⚠

Inefficient Decisions

Lack of standardized insights leads to delayed or inaccurate operational choices.

💸

Unproven AI ROI

Current AI solutions lack deep workflow integration, failing to show business value.

The Objective

Propose a unified platform solution within Jeen.ai that standardizes decision-making. It must allow Builders to easily integrate data sources and enable End-Users to interact with context-aware AI workflows seamlessly in highly secure, on-prem environments.

🔒 On-Prem Technologies

To ensure enterprise-grade security within a closed network:

  • LLMs: Open-weights models (e.g., Llama 3 70B, Mixtral) deployed locally via vLLM.
  • NLP/RAG: HuggingFace sentence transformers for embeddings.
  • Storage: FAISS or Qdrant (On-Prem) for vector retrieval; PostgreSQL for state management.

⚡ AI Automation Engine

How AI improves efficiency:

  • Semantic Normalization: Maps "Employee ID" from HR to "Assignee" in Jira automatically.
  • Agentic Workflows: LangChain-based agents capable of reading BI reports, drafting summaries, and initiating PM tickets in one flow.
